Aluminium Scrap

We are all aware of the infinite recycling properties of aluminium. Recycling aluminium requires about 95% less energy than the production of primary aluminium Aluminium scrap remains a significant resource to be used as raw material to produce secondary aluminum and subsequently new aluminum products. Aluminium scrap can be both 'new' and 'old' scrap. New scrap is generated from aluminum wrought and cast products as the metal is processed by fabricators into consumer or industrial products. Old scrap is retrieved from end-of-life products or discarded products of all types.
